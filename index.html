Why is Soccer the Best Sport in the World?

You have to fight to reach your dream. You have to sacrifice and work hard for it.

The World Cup

The World Cup is a global soccer event that happens every 4 years where the best teams compete with each other to decide who the greatest team is. Last year, in December 2022 Argentina won the competition and Lionel Messi took the Golden Ball naming him the best player of the tournament. The World Cup is the most watched soccer event every year it happens. This past World Cup Final between France and Argentina wracked in 1.5 billion viewers and the stadium sold out.

Champions League

The Champions league is the second biggest soccer event in the world. It happens every year and decides who the greatest soccer club is. The World Cup is international and the Champions League is European Clubs only. The most recent winners are Manchester City who beat Inter Milan to claim the 2022 - 2023 Champions League. The most winning club of the Champions League is Real Madrid with 14 won in their history.

Different Leagues

There are multiple soccer leagues across the world. The U.S. has the MLS (Major League Soccer.) Europe has the Top 5 Leagues in the whole world, The Premiere League, LaLiga, Serie A, Bundesliga, Ligue 1. All these leagues have their own standings and decide a winner at the end of the season. The best teams of the Top 5 Leagues compete in the Champions League.
